Sam's Chicken Burrito

Description

This burrito uses juicy and tender chicken and savory ingredients to deliver an amazing meal. I've created this burrito using the same ingredients I get at Chipotle!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Cilantro-lime rice
  • Pinto beans
  • Salsa verde
  • Corn salsa
  • Monterey jack cheese
  • Tortilla (optional)

Steps

  1. First season the chicken with the spices and then grill it until cooked.
  2. Slice the chicken into bite-sized cubes.
  3. Get your tortilla or bowl and add a layer of rice.
  4. Add a layer of beans and then chicken.
  5. Add your desired amount of salsa verde and corn salsa.
  6. Finally, top it all with a layer of cheese.
