Tasty Toast

Description

You can't go wrong with this breakfast classic. A simple slice of toast with butter is a great way to start the day.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Toaster
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Get a slice of bread and toast it in the toaster.
  2. Once the toaster is finished, take a knife of butter and spread it across the toast.
  3. Enjoy!
